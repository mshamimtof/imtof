Travel and Accommodation

EMBASSY - your preferred travel partner for IMTOF 2024 organized by the MMTMA (Madras Machine Tools Manufacturers Association).

Our travel and accommodation services for exhibitors and attendees:

IInternational Travel Assistance:

Whether you're arriving from a nearby city or across the globe, we're here to assist you in navigating your journey to IMTOF 2024. Chennai is well-connected by air, rail, and road, making it easily accessible for visitors from various locations. Our team can provide guidance on the most convenient travel options and transportation services to ensure a seamless experience.

Accommodation Options:

Discover a range of accommodation options in close proximity to the Chennai Trade Centre, the venue for IMTOF 2024. From luxurious hotels to budget-friendly accommodation, Chennai offers a variety of lodging choices to suit every preference and budget. We recommend booking your accommodation in advance to secure the best rates and availability during your stay.

Local Attractions:

While you're in Chennai for IMTOF 2024, take some time to explore the vibrant city and its cultural attractions. From historical landmarks to bustling markets and delicious cuisine, Chennai offers a wealth of experiences waiting to be discovered. Our team can provide recommendations on must-visit attractions and activities to enhance your visit to the city.
